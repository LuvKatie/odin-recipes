Brown sugar - Boba milk tea

Deliciously sweet in just the right amount paired with fresh milk, brown sugar, and boba soaked in brown sugar syrup.

A drink which taste similar to a milk shake, but it's simplicity brings on a moreish feeling paired with it's fun side-kick, chewy boba balls!

Ingredients:

  • Fresh Milk
  • Boba
  • Brown Sugar

Steps:

  1. Boil Boba in water for 20-35 minutes
  2. Prepare brown sugar alone in a large bowl
  3. Drain boba and while hot pour inside brown sugar bowl
  4. Mix until boba is completely coated in brown sugar syrup
  5. Laddle boba into cusp while drizzling brown sugar on side
  6. Finish with pour of fresh milk, and enjoy!
