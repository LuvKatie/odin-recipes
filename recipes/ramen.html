Spicy Miso Ramen

Delicious umami of Miso paired with the flavorful kick of spice; this egg noodle soup dish will leave you wanting seconds!

Having ramen will usually be customizable in soup or noodle amount, and a variety of toppings. Generally ramen will come with bamboo shoots, seaweed, char siu, a soft boiled egg, and a nice sprink of fresh green onions.

Ingredients:

  • Spicy Miso Paste
  • Shoyu Soft-Boiled Eggs
  • Fatty Ground Pork
  • Toasted Sesame Oil
  • Dried Shitake Mushroom
  • Unsalted Chicken or Pork stock
  • Your choice of servings of fresh Ramen Noodles
  • Nori / Seaweed
  • Finely diced Scallions

Steps:

  1. Prepare Spicy Miso Paste
  2. Marinade Soft-Boiled Eggs in Shoyu
  3. Pan sear Fatty Ground Pork
  4. Mix cooked Pork with Spicy Miso Paste
  5. Add unsalted stock of choice to bowl
  6. Combine all ingredients in bowl
  7. Blanch Fresh Ramen Noodles for 1-2 minutes
  8. Add noodles to bowl and Season with desired Sesame Oil / Shoyu / Spicy Miso Paste
