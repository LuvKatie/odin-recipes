Mashed Potatoes!

Main Page

Golden, smooth, chunky. However you like it mashed potatoes will always be a stable in any steak dinner or just dinner, dinner!

Salty, buttery, cheesy, the best of potato goodness!

Ingredients:

  • Yukon Gold Potatoes
  • Butter
  • Heavy Whipped Cream
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Cheese of choice

Steps:

  1. Bring salted Boield Water to slight boil
  2. Quatered Yukon Gold potatoes ( skin on or off ) into water
  3. Once potatoes are softened, drain.
  4. Mash and salt to taste
  5. Add butter while mashed potatoes are hot
  6. Portion your heavy whipped cream after every mix
  7. Pepper and cheese to your liking!
